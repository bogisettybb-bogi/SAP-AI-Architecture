HANA Vector Engine
Data Foundation
The AI memory capability built inside HANA Cloud. Stores and searches vector embeddings so SAP AI can retrieve relevant knowledge from your company data instantly.
WHAT IT DOES
Stores text, documents, and SAP data as mathematical vectors called embeddings
Performs similarity search to find the most relevant content for any AI query
Powers RAG so LLMs answer questions using your actual company knowledge
WHAT IS RAG?
Retrieval Augmented Generation. Instead of the LLM guessing from its training, it first retrieves relevant documents from HANA Vector Engine and uses those as context to give a precise, grounded answer
REAL EXAMPLES
Policy Q&A bot: 500 HR policy documents stored as vectors. Employee asks about the maternity leave policy in Germany. The vector engine finds the exact relevant section in milliseconds and the LLM answers precisely
Maintenance knowledge: 10 years of equipment maintenance reports stored as vectors. Technician asks what was done last time a pump failed. Vector engine retrieves the relevant historical reports instantly
Contract search: 2,000 supplier contracts stored as vectors. Legal team asks which contracts have a force majeure clause covering pandemics. Answer returned in seconds across all 2,000 documents
WHO USES IT
AI architectsDevelopersData engineers
